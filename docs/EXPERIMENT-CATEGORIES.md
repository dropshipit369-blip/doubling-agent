# First 10 Experiment Categories

1. Digital template flips
2. AI-assisted service offers
3. Affiliate landing page tests
4. Micro SaaS waitlist validation
5. Gumroad digital product launches
6. Prompt pack creation and resale
7. Lead generation offers for local niches
8. Arbitrage on public domain / licensed info packaging
9. Newsletter monetization tests
10. Referral and bounty program experiments

## Selection criteria
Every experiment should be scored by:
- capital required
- time to first result
- execution complexity
- downside risk
- upside potential
- content value
- policy risk

## v1 preference
Start with digital products, services, and affiliate experiments.
Avoid finance-heavy or regulated categories.
