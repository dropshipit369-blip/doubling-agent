## FLIP #1 — Day 1 | Balance: $10 → Target: $20

### SCOUT REPORT: 5 Opportunities

#### Opportunity 1: Canva Template Bundle on Etsy
- **What:** Create a 3-5 template bundle (Instagram posts, resume, planner) in Canva Free, list on Etsy for $5-10
- **How:** (1) Make 3 Canva templates in 2 hours, (2) Export as links, (3) List on Etsy ($0.20 listing fee), (4) Promote in 1-2 relevant Facebook groups
- **Expected return:** $10 cost (Etsy fees + Canva time) → $20-40 from 2-4 sales
- **Why now:** Etsy digital downloads are the #1 trending low-barrier product in 2026. No shipping, instant delivery.
- **Risk:** May not sell on day 1. Etsy SEO takes time to index.

#### Opportunity 2: AI Prompt Pack on Gumroad
- **What:** Create a curated pack of 20-50 ChatGPT/Claude prompts for a specific niche (real estate, fitness, marketing), list for $5-9
- **How:** (1) Pick niche, (2) Write 30 prompts in 1-2 hours, (3) Package as PDF, (4) List on Gumroad (free), (5) Share link on X/Reddit
- **Expected return:** $0 cost → $20+ from 3-4 sales
- **Why now:** Prompt packs are high-demand, zero-cost to create, instant delivery. You already have Gumroad set up.
- **Risk:** Competitive market. Need a sharp niche angle to stand out.

#### Opportunity 3: Thrift Store Flip
- **What:** Buy 1-2 items at a thrift store for $5-10, resell on Mercari/eBay for $20-30
- **How:** (1) Go to Goodwill/thrift, (2) Look for vintage electronics, brand clothing, or collectibles, (3) Check eBay sold prices on phone, (4) Buy items with 2x+ markup, (5) List with photos same day
- **Expected return:** $10 spend → $20-40 resale
- **Why now:** Vintage digital cameras ($10 thrift → $100 eBay), brand backpacks, cast iron — all consistently flip 2x-10x.
- **Risk:** Requires leaving the house. Items may take 3-7 days to sell. Best content option though.

#### Opportunity 4: Micro-Gig Sprint (Fiverr/User Interviews)
- **What:** Complete 1-2 User Interviews studies ($50 avg) or accept a quick Fiverr gig using AI tools
- **How:** (1) Sign up for UserInterviews.com, (2) Apply to 5-10 studies, (3) Complete one 30-min study
- **Expected return:** $0 cost → $30-75 from one study session
- **Why now:** User Interviews pays $50+ per study, same-week payout. Most people qualify within 24 hours.
- **Risk:** May not get accepted to a study on Day 1. Payout takes 1-5 business days.

#### Opportunity 5: Domain Flip
- **What:** Buy a cheap expired domain ($8-10 on Namecheap), list it on Afternic/Sedo for $25-50
- **How:** (1) Search expireddomains.net for short, catchy .com domains, (2) Register for $10, (3) List on Afternic with buy-now price of $50
- **Expected return:** $10 → $25-100 if it sells
- **Why now:** AI-related domains are hot. Anything with "agent", "ai", "gpt" in the name has buyers.
- **Risk:** Domains can sit for weeks/months. Not a 24-hour play unless you find a buyer immediately.

### STRATEGIST DECISION

## THE PLAY: AI Prompt Pack on Gumroad

**Why this one:** Zero downside risk. The $10 stays untouched as reserve capital. You create the product with time (not money), list it for free on Gumroad, and promote it. If it sells 3 copies at $7, you've doubled. If it doesn't sell today, the $10 is still safe and you can pivot tomorrow.

**Execution Plan:**
1. **Pick niche** (30 min): "50 AI Prompts for Solo Founders" — targets your existing audience
2. **Write prompts** (1-2 hours): Business planning, marketing copy, email sequences, social media, product descriptions
3. **Package** (30 min): Clean PDF with table of contents, branded header
4. **List on Gumroad** (15 min): $7 price, title, description, tags
5. **Promote** (30 min): Post on X @clawroomos, share in 1-2 Reddit threads, cross-link from existing products

**Exit criteria:** 3 sales = $21 (doubled). If 0 sales by end of day, flip to Opportunity #3 (thrift flip) tomorrow.

**Fallback:** Thrift store flip on Day 2 if the prompt pack doesn't convert.

**Content hook:** "Day 1: The AI said don't SPEND the $10. Make something with $0 and sell it. Here's what it built in 2 hours."
